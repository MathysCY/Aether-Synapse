System Architecture

This visualization represents a dense neural network graph navigating a 3-dimensional space.

  • Nodes: Represent artificial neurons operating in a parallel high-performance buffer. The volume is constrained to an 800x800x800 spatial sector.
  • Connections: Display pre-calculated synaptic linkages between neurons within a specific distance threshold.
  • AI Path: The glowing bright trail represents active data signals (AI processing) traversing the network connections in real-time.
